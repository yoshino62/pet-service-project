Requirements

Home / Requirements

The health and safety of all pets in our care is our top priority. To ensure a safe environment, we require up-to-date vaccinations for daycare, boarding and training guests.

Vaccination Requirements

To keep all pets safe, required vaccinations must be administered by a licensed veterinarian at least 48 hours before arrival, though we suggest 10 days in advance for the best protection. Proof of vaccination is required, along with compliance with state and local boarding regulations. Please contact your local facility for specific requirements.

Canine:

  • Rabies (1 or 3-yr. vaccine required)
  • DHLPP (1 or 3-yr. vaccine required)
  • Bordetella (Required every 12 months, preferred every six months)
  • Canine Influenza (Dogs that have never received the canine influenza vaccine will require a booster 2-4 weeks after the initial vaccine)

Feline:

  • Rabies
  • FVRCP *Animals under 4 months of age accepted without Rabies vaccine.

Behavior Evaluation For Daycare

To ensure a safe and positive environment for all our furry guests, every dog must undergo a behavioral evaluation before joining our daycare. This assessment helps us understand your dog's personality and ensures they can safely interact with other dogs and our staff. During the evaluation, we check for any signs of aggression, fear, or anxiety that could affect their ability to enjoy group play. Our goal is not to exclude dogs but to create a safe and happy playgroup where every pup can thrive.

Cancellations for Boarding

A 50% deposit is required to secure your pet’s boarding reservation. To receive a full refund, cancellations must be made at least seven days in advance. If a reservation is not canceled in time, the deposit will be forfeited. During peak periods, refunds are not available for early checkouts.
